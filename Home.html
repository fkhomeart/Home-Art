OUR HISTORY

Home Art Block Manufacturing Company was founded in 2013 by Kamil Heyredin, a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for quality construction solutions. The company began its operations with the production of terrazzo tiles, serving the local market with beautifully crafted and durable flooring materials. Over time, as the demand for innovative and reliable construction materials grew, Home Art transitioned into block manufacturing — a move that marked a new era in the company’s journey. Today, Home Art specializes in the production of high-quality concrete blocks using the QTJ4-40 Turkish Block Machine, a modern and efficient technology known for its precision, strength, and consistency. This upgrade has allowed the company to meet the growing needs of contractors, builders, and developers who rely on sturdy materials for long-lasting structures. Under the leadership of Kamil Heyredin, Home Art has built a reputation for excellence, trust, and innovation. Our commitment to customer satisfaction, timely delivery, and continuous improvement drives everything we do. As we continue to grow, we remain dedicated to offering reliable building solutions that support the development of stronger and more beautiful communities.

We understand that each building project comes with its own unique requirements. That’s why we offer a versatile range of concrete blocks suited for various structural and architectural applications. Our most commonly produced block sizes are 10 cm, 15 cm, and 20 cm, each designed with specific uses in mind. Whether a project involves internal partition walls, structural load-bearing walls, or foundational work, our block dimensions provide builders with the flexibility and strength they need to achieve lasting results. The dimensional accuracy of our blocks also ensures faster installation, reduces the need for excessive mortar, and contributes to overall construction efficiency. What sets our products apart is not just their durability, but the consistency with which they are made. We apply strict quality control measures during every stage of production, from raw material preparation to the final curing process. This ensures that every block leaving our facility maintains uniform shape, density, and compressive strength. Such consistency is especially important in large-scale projects where uniformity plays a major role in structural integrity and visual appeal. In addition to standard sizes, we are open to working with contractors and developers who may require custom solutions for special projects. Our team is always ready to consult, advise, and tailor production to meet specific structural challenges or design needs.
